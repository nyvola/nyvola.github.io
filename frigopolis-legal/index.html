Frigopolis — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-10

Frigopolis is a self-hosted family pantry-inventory app published by Nyvola Studio. The app works fully offline; connecting to a household server is optional and that server is hosted by you or your family. Nyvola Studio operates no central Frigopolis server and has no access to your inventory, account or photos.

Data we handle

  • Advertising (free version): the app shows banner ads via Google AdMob, which collects a device advertising identifier to serve ads. In the EEA/UK a consent form (Google UMP) is shown on first launch.
  • Crash diagnostics: Firebase Crashlytics collects anonymous crash reports (stack traces, device model, OS) to help fix bugs.
  • Your content (pantry items, photos, and, only if you connect a server, your email and display name): stored locally on your device and, if you opt in, on your own self-hosted server. Nyvola Studio never receives it.

No other marketing or analytics tracking. Scanned barcodes are sent over HTTPS to Open Food Facts / UPCitemdb only to fetch product info.

Deleting your account and data

  • In the app: My account → Delete my account permanently deletes your account and personal data from the household server.
  • Without the app, or any request: email nyvolastudio@gmail.com. As the server is self-hosted, the household administrator can also delete any account and wipe all data.

Frigopolis — Politique de confidentialité (FR)

Frigopolis est une application d'inventaire familial auto-hébergée, éditée par Nyvola Studio. Elle fonctionne hors ligne ; la connexion à un serveur est optionnelle et ce serveur est hébergé par vous. Nyvola Studio n'exploite aucun serveur central et n'a accès à aucune de vos données.

  • Publicité (version gratuite) : bannières via Google AdMob (identifiant publicitaire ; formulaire de consentement RGPD au 1er lancement).
  • Diagnostics de plantage : Firebase Crashlytics (rapports de crash anonymes).
  • Vos données (articles, photos, et — seulement si vous connectez un serveur — email et nom) : stockées sur votre appareil et, si vous le choisissez, sur votre propre serveur. Nyvola Studio ne les reçoit jamais.

Suppression : dans l'app via Mon compte → Supprimer mon compte, ou par email à nyvolastudio@gmail.com.
